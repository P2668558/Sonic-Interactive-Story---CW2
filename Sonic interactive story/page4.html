Amy

CLICK IMAGE

Amy Rose is a video game character in the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise. Kazuyuki Hoshino developed her, and she initially debuted in the game Sonic CD in 1993. Amy is a pink anthropomorphic hedgehog known for her Sonic obsession. She is frequently shown as a cheery, optimistic character with a strong sense of justice.

Amy was initially portrayed as a damsel in distress who was continuously kidnapped by Dr. Eggman. Her character, however, matured with time into a more independent and capable heroine. She has been in a number of Sonic games and is frequently portrayed as a talented fighter who is prepared to battle for what is right. Despite her obsession with Sonic, Amy is a popular character among Sonic fans.
